What data do we store? We have tried to make this post easily read in plain English.

We DO NOT store your password for myMCPS or Google Classroom. Here is a list of information we do store on our servers. The italics is the reason we choose to indefiniently keep this information.
  • Your ID Number - To remember who has used this site. We also associate your other data with this ID number.
  • Some or all of the assignments you have accessed via this site, from this quarter or past quarters, accessed via myMCPS or any other connected platform - To display the assignments you have 'checked' off, and to allow this data to be persistent between visits.
